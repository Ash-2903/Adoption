Know everything about pet grooming

With proper pet grooming, you will get rid of shedding, fleas, ticks, and various health conditions. Proper grooming is more than just having a cute pet. By grooming your pet, you will also be tackling potentially unhealthy conditions. It will also allow you to identify any underlying conditions or diseases early. Therefore, you will be able to take your furry friend for treatment sooner and more efficiently.

Grooming

Grooming helps in checking for flea/parasite, addressing fur matting, identifying dermatologic issues, soothing medicated shampoos for clean, shiny, fur, tracking weight, keeping your cat’s nails trimmed and ear cleaning. How much grooming a dog needs depends on its breed, age, and health. Regular grooming helps to ensure the dog is healthy and comfortable. Daily grooming decreases chance of various health problems, such as thrush, scratches, and other skin problems.

For Dogs

Dog grooming refers to both the hygienic care and cleaning of a dog, as well as a process by which a dog's physical appearance is enhanced for showing or other types of competition. Grooming is a vital part in the well-being and healthiness of a dog, which can improve its quality of life.

For Cats

By nature, cats are well equipped to groom themselves. Cats groom themselves to try to rid of any scents that may let other animals know they are around. Grooming also helps distribute their natural oils to make their fur shiny and clean. One of the great advantages of grooming at is that we make it completely stress-free.
